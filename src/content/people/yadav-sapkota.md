He focuses on childhood cancer survivorship and uses analytical and experimental methods to understand why some survivors develop late effects after treatment.

His work brings together big-data modeling, multi-omics, and patient-specific human induced pluripotent stem cell systems to study cardiotoxicity, diabetes, obesity, hearing loss, neurotoxicity, and other treatment-related outcomes.
