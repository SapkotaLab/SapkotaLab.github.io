The lab studies how genetic associations differ across ancestry groups and why those differences matter for prediction and care.

This work is aimed at making survivorship models more generalizable so that clinical tools are useful beyond European-ancestry cohorts.

The emphasis is on equitable risk estimation rather than one-size-fits-all assumptions.
