Functional work complements statistical discovery by asking how a variant changes cellular response to therapy.

The lab uses patient-specific hiPSC-derived cells to study cardiotoxicity, cisplatin-related hearing loss, methotrexate neurotoxicity, and other treatment effects in a tissue-relevant way.

This line of work helps separate signals that are statistically associated from signals that are mechanistically actionable.
