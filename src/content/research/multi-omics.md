This theme integrates whole-genome sequencing, whole-exome sequencing, RNA sequencing, proteomics, and metabolomics.

The goal is to connect molecular signals with downstream clinical outcomes and identify interpretable markers that can be validated in survivor cohorts.

The lab also studies ancestry-specific genetic effects and other sources of heterogeneity in survivor populations.
